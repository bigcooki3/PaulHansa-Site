ABOUT

My name's Paul Hansa. I'm a computer science major at Georgia State University, but I'm not just a code monkey. I carry experience in programming, photography, video editing and effects, graphic design, game development, and full stack web development. I pride myself in easily adapting to new tools and platforms, and evolving around the fleeting, dynamic nature of technology used in the industry by carrying long-tested programming concepts and idealogies with me.

My work experience has carried me through many doors — I've worked as a web developer in the Atlanta Tech Village, a thriving community of tech startups and entrepreneur, as well a photographer at a professional studio and a director of the University of Georgia's annual hackathon, UGAHacks 3. Although I'm a full-time computer science student, I'm also currently working as a Web Application Developer at an Atlanta-based marketing, data and technology company called SimplePart, serving international automotive manufacturers.
